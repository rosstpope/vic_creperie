Forget pancakes & small portions.
It's all about Crepes, filled to the brim.

At Victoria Creperie deeply passionate about elevating the humble crepe to glorious new heights. We love the theatre of making something delicious infront of our customers and giving them the freshest and tastiest ingredients to be topped, poured, dripped, sprinkled or dollopped to make your dream dessert. ‘I want something savoury’ we hear you say. We’ve got you and your crepe covered!

There’s more to us than cooking great food. We’re also good at listening. So if you have ideas about what you want or you’re struggling to find a caterer willing to listen, then here are just a few ways we can work with you.

Corporate events

Whether it's a staff party or you're putting on an event for guests, we can plate up some of the tastiest food to keep your guests happy and the conversations flowing.
Our team can set up inside or outside a venue and proide plates for all your guests. The best thing is we don't need curlery, you can eat it anywhere. If your guests are messy eaters then we've got plenty of napkins for them to keep their suits looking sharp.
